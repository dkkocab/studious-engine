Sharks in Hell

Local 928 owner Mark Haddlesey has started a new tradition of inviting 928 owners to his cottage in rural Hell, Michigan. Owners and friends from sometimes far away places make the journey to this small town to enjoy Mark's hospitality and good company. It's a weekend event in August of each year, and there's lots of room for us to have a picnic (and even camp overnight). The annual "Helluva Cruise" charts its course right past Mark's front door, so we all get to enjoy a parade of custom cars, bikes and hot rods. Our group has its own fun run through the rural countryside.
