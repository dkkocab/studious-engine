Before sending off the cam covers and intake for powder coating, I wanted to remove all the the oil fittings and other pieces attached to everything. The passenger side cam cover has a hollow plastic cup attached to the underside call the "oil separator."



The oil separator is secured to the underside with a nut that is locked in place by a washer. To release the washer, just get a long thin screwdriver and bend the tab back. The nut, however, can be pretty tight. It has 4 notches cut into it rather than a hex-shaped head.
