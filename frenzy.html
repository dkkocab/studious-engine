The 2003 Fresh Water Shark Frenzy

The Detroit area 928 owners hosted a FRESH WATER SHARK FRENZY on July 12, 2003 at the Dexter-Huron Metropark near Ann Arbor, Michigan. Porsche 928 owners from as far as Connecticut and Georga made the trip. Here are a few photos of the picnic. This event was the predecessor to our annual Sharks in Hell event.
